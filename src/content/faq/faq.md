---
title: Frequently Asked Questions
questions:
  - question: What time should I arrive?
    answer: |
      Please arrive 30 minutes before the ceremony begins to ensure you have time 
      to find your seat and get settled.
  - question: What is the dress code?
    answer: |
      We request cocktail attire. Please avoid wearing white, cream, or ivory colors.
  - question: Can I bring a plus one?
    answer: |
      Plus ones are welcome if indicated on your invitation. Please RSVP with the 
      exact number of guests attending.
  - question: Will the ceremony and reception be indoors or outdoors?
    answer: |
      The ceremony will be held outdoors, weather permitting. The reception will be 
      indoors. Please check the weather and dress accordingly.
  - question: Is there parking available?
    answer: |
      Yes, complimentary parking is available at the venue. Valet service will also 
      be provided.
  - question: What if I have dietary restrictions?
    answer: |
      Please indicate any dietary restrictions or allergies when you RSVP. We will 
      do our best to accommodate all dietary needs.
---
